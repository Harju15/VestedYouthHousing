What do we offer? Thats the fundemental question!

01.

We offer a safe space.

We know when the youth are transitioning out of foster care it can be a hard time. Being thrust into a world you have not been prepared for can be scary. That is why our transitional home provides a safe and comfroting space where you can take things slow and get adjusted.

Learn more
02.

We offer life skills.

We want the youth to experience what is like living independenly. We want to show them how to manage money and take care of themselves, all while having a safety net if things get hard.

Learn more
03.

We offer compassion and understanding.

Life is hard! we know that life does not always go how we want it to go and we understand that. that is why we offer a place for the youth to learn the twists and turns of life and how to cope with them without being exposed to the harsh consequences of life

Learn more

Our goal is to help improve the outlook of foster kids who are forced out into the real world they were not prepared for.

We will do everything in our power to help the youth have a smooth transition to independent and succesful lives.

Why is it important!

The harsh reality of the numbers.

11,500

As of November 30, 2023, there were almost 10,500 children 17 and younger in foster care in North Carolina, and closer to 11,500 when including children 18 to 21 years old.
- Spectrum News

20% HOMELESS,
50% UNEMPLOYED

A staggering 20% of all foster kids end up homeless in North carolina alone. On top of that 50%, yes half, of all foster children are unemployed. These numbers show the great need for transtional housing in NC!
- cfknc.org
